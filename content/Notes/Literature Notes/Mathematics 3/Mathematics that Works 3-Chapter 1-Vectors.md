### 2.1 Solving Systems of Linear Equations Systematically

#### 2.1.1 Systems of Linear Equations
Assume the following linear equation with two unknowns, $x$ and $y$

$$
\left\{
\begin{array}{ll}
0.80x + 0.10y &= 30 \\
0.10x + 0.70y &= 45 \\
\end{array}
\right
$$
