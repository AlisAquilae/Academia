### 2.1 Matrices and their Operations
#### 2.1.1 Rows
If 

$$
Q=
\begin{pmatrix}
q_1 & q_2 & q_3 & q_4
\end{pmatrix}
, x=
\begin{pmatrix}
x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4
\end{pmatrix}
$$
we can compute the **product** $Qx$ as 

$$
\begin{aligned}
Qx &=
\begin{pmatrix}
q_1 & q_2 & q_3 & q_4
\end{pmatrix}

\begin{pmatrix}
x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \\ x_4
\end{pmatrix} \\
&= q_1x_1+q_2x_2+q_3x_3+

\end{aligned}

$$
