### 7.1 Graph and Tangent Plant
In this chapter we study functions of two variables. We make computations in $R^2$ and $R^3$. To keep the notation simple we indicate the coordinates of a point by $x$ and $y$ (and $z$). Note that the results of this chapter can readily be generalised to functions of $n$
